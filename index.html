Introduction

Welcome to my homepage! I'm Isaac, currently a student in the University of California, Berkeley Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department starting on my first year in the PhD program. My research focus is on Embedded Systems, and I’m in Professor Edward Lee's CHESS (Center for Hybrid and Embedded Software Systems) group. I obtained my B.S. for Computer Engineering at UCSB. I like playing and watching basketball, enjoy playing guitar and some occasional web design. I love Jesus and believe that he is my savior and friend! I was part of Asian American Christian Fellowship in UCSB, and Good Shepard Christian Church in Fremont, CA. Enjoy your stay here, and feel free to leave a message for me on my message board here, or you can contact me here if you have any questions! !
